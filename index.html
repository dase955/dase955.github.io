Hello 👋, DaSE955 Lab focuses on modern database research, with YANG Chengcheng as its head. The main research interests include but are not limited to database storage systems, AI for databases, query optimization and so on.


Lab Overview

We are the DaSE955 Lab at School of Data Science and Engineering, East China Normal University (ECNU). Our research focuses on the application of machine learning techniques to various problems in database management, with the vision of building an intelligent, self-adpative data management system. In summary, our current research includes:

  • Data Storage (LSM-tree related)
  • Query Optimizer (Learned Cardinality Estimation)
  • Set Similarity Join
  • Machine Learning for Database
